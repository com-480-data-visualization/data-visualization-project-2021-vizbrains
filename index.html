Timeline

Since the 1920s, music changed dramatically. The 20th century is actually described as the “age of musical diversity”. Nowadays the streaming platforms dominate the industry, and allow us to bring together all these musical works.

So what were the most popular songs throughout the years and how popular were they ? On the timeline below, we selected for each year the most popular song according to Spotify statistics.

Feel free to navigate on the timeline and listen to the samples.
• Scroll on the graph to zoom in or out. The more you zoom in, the more years will be visible.
• Navigate through the timeline with the horizontal scroll bar, or by dragging the cursor directly on the graph.
